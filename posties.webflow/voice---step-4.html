Write in your voice
Below are a few guided writing prompts. Complete at least 2 the way you would actually post them. We're learning how you naturally express ideas across different kinds of LinkedIn posts.
Take a Stance
Write a post with a strong opinion on something you care about. Pick something you genuinely believe and say it the way you actually would on LinkedIn.
Teach Something
Write a post explaining a trend in your space to smart non-experts. Aim to make the idea clear without dumbing it down. Show how you naturally explain things.
Personal Insight
Write a post about a lesson, pattern, or belief shaped by your experience. This can be reflective, opinionated, or story-driven, whatever feels most natural to you.
Share Something
Write a post celebrating news, a milestone, or an announcement. Think about how you naturally share wins, signal enthusiasm, and add context or meaning.
